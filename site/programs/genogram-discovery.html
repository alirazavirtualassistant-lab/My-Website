Genogram Discovery Program

Uncover the generational patterns behind your behaviors — and chart a new path.

What is a genogram?

The Myers-Morrison Genogram Discovery Program is designed to uncover patterns of behavior that occur across generations, offering insight into the source of your pain, fears, compulsions, maladaptive patterns, or unconscious motivations. Working directly with Cynthia through a series of one-hour sessions, you'll document a personalized genogram to understand the past and construct a road map for your future.

What's included

  • A series of one-on-one, one-hour sessions with Cynthia
  • A personalized genogram mapping your family relationships and patterns
  • Insight into hereditary and psychological influences on your behavior
  • A road map to release inherited difficulties and move toward your goals

Who this is for

  • Anyone trying to change something they feel stuck in
  • People who want more understanding of their unconscious inclinations
  • Those ready to alter the course of their future

More about it

A genogram (pronounced jen-uh-gram) is a graphic representation of a family tree that displays detailed data on relationships among individuals. It goes beyond a traditional family tree by letting you analyze hereditary patterns and psychological factors that shape relationships — quickly revealing influences that a simple pedigree chart would miss.
