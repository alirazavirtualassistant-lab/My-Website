Food Addiction Coaching

Personalized one-on-one recovery coaching, co-created around you.

What is Food Addiction Coaching?

After completing the SUGAR® Assessment, Cynthia offers personalized, one-on-one recovery coaching. Together, you'll co-create an individualized program focused on your goals, budget, schedule, and preferences. The listed price is a starting suggestion — your actual cost is determined with Cynthia, who will tailor the schedule, timing, and frequency to you.

What's included

  • One-on-one coaching with a trained food addiction professional
  • New habits, perspectives, and ways to manage your relationship with food
  • A focus on prevention — planning ahead for food-centered situations
  • Support meal-to-meal and day-to-day, delivered online, by phone, or email

Who this is for

  • People who've completed the SUGAR® Assessment and want ongoing support
  • Anyone struggling with obsession around food, eating, or weight
  • Those who want to be free of self-judgment and shame

More about it

Many people suffer from obsession with food and eating behaviors — dieting, overeating, undereating, bingeing and purging, or constant thoughts about food and weight — without recognizing these may be symptoms of addiction. Working with a trained coach, you'll develop new habits and ways to manage your relationship with food, eating, and yourself, with a focus on prevention and a recovery-supporting lifestyle.
