What is the SUGAR® Assessment?

The SUGAR® Assessment is a clinical tool used to identify and examine the harmful and pathological use of sugar and food. It is built on extensive research showing that sugar and food addiction is a condition identical to alcohol, narcotic, and drug disorders — a brain illness, not a lack of willpower or a reflection of weakness or poor character.

What's included

  • A structured, one-on-one assessment with Cynthia
  • Access to the 13-section SUGAR® Pre-Assessment Evaluation (PAE)
  • A determination of “harmful use” vs. “pathological use” (addiction)
  • Your personal addiction development curve, mapped chronologically
  • A clear, compassionate path toward next steps

Who this is for

  • Anyone who suspects sugar, flour, or ultra-processed foods control them
  • People exhausted by diets, misdiagnoses, and programs that didn't work
  • Those ready for a science-based, judgment-free starting point

More about it

Recent research shows sugar, flour, and highly processed foods can act as psychoactive drugs on the reward system — especially for those predisposed to addiction. The assessment results are a strong motivational tool, clinically shown to increase compliance with a recovery protocol.
