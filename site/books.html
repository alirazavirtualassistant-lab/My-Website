Read your way to freedom

Cynthia Myers-Morrison, EdD is the co-author of books that help adults and families understand cravings and nourish themselves with real food.

Bestselling guide

The Fix for Cravings

One(S) That Didn't Work, and Now One Which Works. Cynthia's flagship book blends the science of food and sugar addiction with the lived story of lasting recovery — a compassionate, practical guide for anyone ready to find freedom from cravings.

What you'll gain

  • An understanding of why willpower alone fails
  • The brain science behind sugar, flour, and processed-food cravings
  • A real, workable path to abstinence and peace with food
