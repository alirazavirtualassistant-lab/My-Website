Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: 2026. This is a template policy and should be reviewed by qualified counsel before launch.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly — such as your name and email when you join our newsletter, purchase a program, or contact us — and limited technical data (cookies, analytics) to improve the site.

How we use it

To deliver programs and content you request, respond to inquiries, send newsletters you opt into, and improve our services. We do not sell your personal information.

Payments

Purchases are completed on the official myersmorrison.com store, where payments are processed through Wix’s PCI-compliant secure checkout. We do not store your full card details on this website.

Health information

Assessment and intake forms may collect sensitive health information. Such data is handled with care and, where applicable, through HIPAA-compliant tools. It is used solely to provide the services you request.

Your choices

You may unsubscribe from emails at any time and request access to or deletion of your data by contacting cynthiajmm@gmail.com.
