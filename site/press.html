Short bio (50 words)

Cynthia Myers-Morrison, EdD brings a history as a therapist in private practice with decades of abstinence from grains and sugars after recovery from alcohol and drugs. With a Doctorate in Education and three Master's degrees, she co-authored The Fix for Cravings, administers the Genogram Discovery Program, speaks, educates, and advocates on recovery.
